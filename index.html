I am broadly interested in reasoning in language models, specifically:

  • How it is acquired: I study toy models of LLMs to understand the learning dynamics and inductive biases that enable generalization
  • How to evaluate it: I aim to develop principled methods to assess reasoning in large pretrained models, using tools from interpretability and carefully designed experiments.
  • How to improve it: I explore how RL finetuning and objective function design affect reasoning capabilities.
